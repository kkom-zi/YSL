FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  1. 1. HOW DO I RETURN A PRODUCT?

    YOU CAN RETURN YOUR ONLINE PURCHASE WITHIN 30 DAYS FROM THE DELIVERY OR PICK UP DATE. RETURNS ARE COMPLIMENTARY AND OUR ORDERS COME WITH A PREPAID RETURN LABEL.

    EXTENDED RETURN POLICY UNTIL JANUARY 31ST FOR ORDERS PLACED BETWEEN NOVEMBER 14TH AND DECEMBER 31ST

    PLEASE NOTE, SAINT LAURENT IS NOT LIABLE FOR MERCHANDISE RETURNED THROUGH AN ALTERNATIVE CARRIER SERVICE, WE RECOMMEND USING THE PREPAID LABEL.

    ALL RETURNS MUST BE RETURNED IN THE COUNTRY OF PURCHASE. FOR MORE INFORMATION ON OUR RETURN POLICY AND PRODUCTS THAT ARE ELIGIBLE FOR RETURN, PLEASE VISIT OUR RETURN POLICY.

    TO PROCESS A RETURN, PLEASE FOLLOW THE STEPS BELOW :

    1. 1. FILL OUT THE ONLINE RETURN FORM BY CLICKING ON ""RETURNS"" IN YOUR ORDER HISTORY IN MY ACCOUNT
    2. 2. MAKE SURE YOUR ITEM(S) ARE RETURNED IN THEIR ORIGINAL CONDITION AND WITH ALL TAGS ATTACHED.
    3. 3. PLACE THE ITEM(S) IN THE ORIGINAL PACKAGING AND SHIPPING BOX AND ATTACH THE ENCLOSED RETURN SHIPPING LABEL OVER THE INITIAL DELIVERY LABEL.
    4. 4. CONTACT FEDEX TO ARRANGE A PICK-UP AT YOUR CONVENIENCE OR DROP OFF YOUR RETURN AT ONE OF THE CARRIERS ACCESS POINTS.

    FOR MORE INFORMATION ON THE CARRIER AND ITS ACCESS POINT LOCATIONS, PLEASE CONTACT OUR CLIENT SERVICE OR VISIT THE FEDEX WEBSITE.

    IF YOU WISH TO RETURN YOUR ITEM IN STORE, PLEASE VISIT THE STORE LOCATOR ON YSL.COM TO FIND THE NEAREST STORE. ON BEHALF OF OUR CLIENTS, FREE STANDING STORES WILL FACILITATE A RETURN BY SHIPPING PARCELS BACK TO OUR WAREHOUSE. UPON ARRIVAL AND ACCEPTANCE OF THE RETURNED PARCEL AND MERCHANDISE A REFUND WILL BE ADMINISTERED. KINDLY NOTE, RETURNS ARE NOT POSSIBLE IN DEPARTMENT STORES OR OUTLETS LOCATIONS.

  2. 2. CAN I ASK FOR AN EXCHANGE?

    FOR EXCHANGE INQUIRIES, PLEASE CONTACT THE CLIENT SERVICE.

  3. 3. I LOST MY RETURN LABEL. HOW CAN I GET A NEW ONE?

    IF YOU LOST THE PRE-PAID RETURN LABEL, PLEASE CONTACT US

  4. 4. CAN I RECEIVE MY REFUND ON A CREDIT CARD THAT IS DIFFERENT FROM THE ONE I USED FOR THE ORDER?

    FOR SECURITY REASONS, WE CANNOT REFUND A CREDIT CARD THAT IS DIFFERENT FROM THE ONE USED FOR THE PURCHASE.

  5. 5. WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY REFUND?

    YOUR RETURN WILL BE PROCESSED WITHIN 5 TO 7 BUSINESS DAYS AFTER THE ITEM(S) HAVE BEEN RECEIVED BY THE SAINT LAURENT TEAM. ONCE YOUR RETURN HAS BEEN ACCEPTED, YOUR REFUND WILL BE ISSUED WITHIN 7 BUSINESS DAYS. A CONFIRMATION WILL BE SEND TO YOU BY EMAIL ONCE THE REFUND HAS BEEN COMPLETED.

    THE TIME NEEDED TO PROCESS YOUR REFUND MAY VARY BASED ON THE PAYMENT METHOD USED :

    CREDIT CARD :

    OUR BANK WILL REIMBURSE YOU WITHIN 7 BUSINESS DAYS OF THE RETURN ACCEPTANCE CONFIRMATION EMAIL. THE TIME IT TAKES TO RESTORE THE CREDIT BACK TO YOUR ACCOUNT DEPENDS ON THE CREDIT CARD ISSUER.

    PAYPAL :

    YOU WILL SEE THE REFUND IN YOUR PAYPAL ACCOUNT WITHIN 24 HOURS OF THE RETURN ACCEPTANCE CONFIRMATION EMAIL.

  6. 6. WHERE CAN I CHECK THE RETURN STATUS?

    YOU CAN CHECK YOUR RETURN STATUS IN MY ACCOUNT SECTION OR BY CLICKING ON RETURNS IN THE FOOTER OF THE WEBSITE.

  7. 7. I DECIDED TO KEEP MY ORDER, BUT HAVE ALREADY COMPLETED THE RETURN FORM.

    WE ARE GLAD YOU DECIDED TO KEEP YOUR PURCHASE. YOU DON'T HAVE TO DO ANYTHING AND THERE IS NO NEED TO CONTACT US.
