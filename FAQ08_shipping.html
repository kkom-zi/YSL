FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  1. 1. WHAT ARE THE SHIPPING METHODS AND COST?

    ALL YSL.COM ORDERS ARE GIFT WRAPPED IN SIGNATURE PACKAGING AND SHIPPED SECURELY IN A CARDBOARD BOX.

    STANDARD SHIPPING
    • AVERAGE DELIVERY TIME: 3 - 6 WORKING DAYS
    • COMPLIMENTARY

    FEDEX IS OUR COURIER FOR ALL SHIPMENTS. YOU WILL RECEIVE AN EMAIL CONTAINING YOUR TRACKING NUMBER ONCE YOUR PACKAGE HAS BEEN SHIPPED

    PLEASE BE INFORMED THE ITEMS WITHIN YOUR ORDER MAY BE SHIPPED SEPARATELY FROM DIFFERENT LOCATIONS AT NO ADDITIONAL COST. YOU WILL RECEIVE AN EMAIL AS SOON AS EACH PARCEL IS SHIPPED WITH THE ASSOCIATED TRACKING NUMBER. PLEASE CONTACT OUR CLIENT SERVICE SHOULD YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS.

    FOR SECURITY REASONS, SAINT LAURENT ONLINE PURCHASES WILL REQUIRE A SIGNATURE UPON DELIVERY. PLEASE NOTE THAT FEDEX WILL DELIVER THE PARCEL TO CUSTOMERS OVER THE LEGAL AGE OF 21.

    UPON DELIVERY, PLEASE MAKE SURE THAT THE NUMBER OF BOXES DELIVERED ARE AS STATED ON THE SHIPPING CONFIRMATION EMAIL, AND THAT THE PACKAGE IS NOT DAMAGED IN ANY WAY. ANY DAMAGE TO THE PACKAGING AND/OR THE PRODUCT OR ANY DISCREPANCY BETWEEN THE NUMBER OF BOXES DELIVERED SHOULD BE IMMEDIATELY NOTIFIED BY INDICATING IN WRITING ON THE COURIER’S DELIVERY DOCUMENTS.

    OUR COURIER DELIVERS FROM MONDAY TO FRIDAY DURING BUSINESS HOURS AND WILL MAKE THREE DELIVERY ATTEMPTS. AFTER THE THIRD FAILED ATTEMPT, YOUR ORDER WILL BE RETURNED TO OUR OFFICES. PLEASE NOTE THAT ONCE YOU HAVE PLACED AN ORDER, IT IS NO LONGER POSSIBLE TO MODIFY YOUR SHIPPING ADDRESS.

  2. 2. CAN I PICK UP MY ORDER IN A STORE?

    UNFORTUNATELY, THIS SERVICE IS NOT YET AVAILABLE. PLEASE DO NOT HESITATE TO CONTACT OUR CLIENT SERVICE SHOULD YOU NEED ANY ASSISTANCE.

  3. 3. CAN I CHOOSE A DELIVERY DATE/HOUR?

    UNFORTUNATELY, IT IS NOT POSSIBLE TO CHOOSE A SPECIFIC DATE AND HOUR. YOU CAN TRY TO CONTACT FEDEX DIRECTLY TO ORGANIZE A POSSIBLE DELIVERY DATE.

  4. 4. WHEN WILL MY ORDER BE SHIPPED?

    YOU WILL RECEIVE AN EMAIL AS SOON AS YOUR ORDER HAS BEEN SHIPPED.

  5. 5. WHERE CAN I CHECK THE STATUS OF MY ORDER?

    YOU CAN CHECK THE STATUS OF YOUR ORDER AND TRACK ITS DELIVERY AT ANY TIME BY LOGGING IN YOUR PERSONAL ACCOUNT OR BY CLICKING ON TRACK ORDER IN THE FOOTER OF THE WEBSITE.

  6. 6. WHAT SHOULD I DO IF MY ORDER HASN'T BEEN DELIVERED WITHIN THE ESTIMATED TIME?

    IF YOUR ORDER HAS NOT BEEN DELIVERED WITHIN THE ESTIMATED PERIOD, WE SUGGEST THAT YOU:

    • CHECK THE ORDER STATUS IN YOUR PERSONAL ACCOUNT
    • CHECK THAT THE ADDRESS INDICATED FOR THE DELIVERY IS CORRECT

    IN THE CASE OF A MISSED DELIVERY, CONTACT FEDEX. FOR FURTHER ASSISTANCE, CONTACT OUR CLIENT SERVICE.

  7. 7. ARE THERE ANY SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS?

    AT THIS TIME WE ARE UNABLE TO SHIP ORDERS TO GENERAL DELIVERY, P.O. BOXES, VIRGIN ISLANDS AND PUERTO RICO.

    ORDERS MADE TO ANY OF THESE ADDRESSES WILL BE CANCELLED.

    THE DELIVERY ADDRESS OF YOUR ORDER MUST MATCH THE COUNTRY SITE IN WHICH YOU PLACE YOUR ORDER. IF YOU WOULD LIKE YOUR ORDER TO BE DELIVERED TO A DIFFERENT COUNTRY, PLEASE SELECT YOUR PREFERRED SHIPPING COUNTRY ON THE MENU BAR BEFORE PLACING YOUR ORDER.

  8. 8. HOLIDAY CALENDAR

    OUR WAREHOUSE WILL BE CLOSED ON:

    • JANUARY 1ST
    • NOVEMBER 23RD
    • DECEMBER 25TH

    PLEASE PLAN ACCORDINGLY AS ORDERS WILL NOT BE SHIPPED OUT ON THESE DATES. ALL ORDERS ARE PROCESSED AUTOMATICALLY AND WE ARE UNABLE TO EXPEDITE OR DELAY SHIPPING TIMES.

  9. 9. WHAT CAN I DO IF MY PARCEL IS DAMAGED?

    UPON DELIVERY, PLEASE MAKE SURE THAT THE NUMBER OF BOXES DELIVERED ARE AS STATED ON THE SHIPPING CONFIRMATION EMAIL, AND THAT THE PACKAGE IS NOT DAMAGED IN ANY WAY. ANY DAMAGE TO THE PACKAGING AND/OR THE PRODUCT OR ANY DISCREPANCY BETWEEN THE NUMBER OF BOXES DELIVERED SHOULD BE IMMEDIATELY NOTIFIED BY INDICATING THE CONDITION OF THE PARCEL (DAMAGED PACKAGE, OPEN BOX...) IN WRITING ON THE COURIER’S DELIVERY DOCUMENTS, UNDER YOUR SIGNATURE.
